Orientation Checklist

This was a page I built while at Arts & Science IT. It was a checklist of items that students needed to complete before they attended orientation. All information was then tied back into the larger advising portal.

The site was used for all incoming freshmen students admitted for Fall 2011 and Fall 2012 throughout CU. This was an estimated 8,000 students per season.
