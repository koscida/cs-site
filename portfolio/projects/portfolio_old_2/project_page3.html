Magic Mailbox

This was a project I worked on in GEEN 1400: Freshmen Projects. I worked in a group with 9 other students creating an oversized mailbox that utalized a touchscreen tablet for kids ages 4-7 to take and send pictures with. I was the lead programmer on the project and was responsible for creating the GUI as well.

Simplicity and visuals drove my design for the interface, which was especially key since I was creating a product that was going to be used by an audience with very limited reading capibilities. We did user testing with a small group of children which was incredibly informative to our design.

I built the project using labview, which was a challenging yet fun experience because I had to teach myself everything and learn to work around the limits of the language. The project was submitted at the Engineering Design Expo and won Best in Group and Runner-up for User's Choice Award.
