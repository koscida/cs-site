About Me
 
My name is Brittany Kos and I’m a junior in Computer Science at the University of Colorado at Boulder. I plan to graduate in December of 2012 with my Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science and a minor in Technology, Arts, and Media.
I have always been interested in design and color as well as problem solving and engineering. (It became a problem when I wanted to go to art school and my mom would only support engineering). As I have grown up, it has always been this constant battle between the left and right halves of my brain fighting for dominance. I ultimately chose to study computer science engineering, but with a human-centered computing emphasis. I eventually want to design and build systems for people to use on any technological platform.
Lately I have been getting into more design and layout of systems and have been working heavily with websites. I started teaching myself html and css a few months ago, so my projects are still at a beginner’s stage, but I plan to expand on what I have learned and continue working with web technology.
