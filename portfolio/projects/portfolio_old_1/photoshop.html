Sequential Images


This was an assignment for my Digital Media 1 class I completed recently. The requirements were to create two images in photoshop from scanned images, pictures from the web, and pictures we took, and made a collage of two images that told a story. The idea behind mine was that we often think smart phones, such as the iPhone are great inventions, but somethings we want them to do practical things (like take a nap) or awesome things (like use the force).
