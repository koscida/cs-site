This is the portal I used for ATLS 3010, Digital Media 2. The semester was spent learning web basics and Adobe software such as Photoshop, Dreamweaver, and Flash.

Though the site has changed radically from its initial design, the content has stayed the same. Some of the previous designs are on the right.

Photoshop

During this semester I was introduced to Photoshop and photo editing for the first time. My work here is still very amateurish, as I am just learning how to work the software. I have no background in design, but my hope is to gain an understanding of how the program works for future projects. After that, I will start work on improving my design skills.

Sequential Images

For this project we had to create two pictures that told a story with just their images. We were required to use one scanned image, a digital image, and an image we created in the final product.

Self Portrait

The Self Portrait was a Photoshop project designed to teach more indepth about the different tools and functions the program has. The assignment was to put our face in a factious background. The picture was required to be 3750 x 4840 and 300 dpi.
