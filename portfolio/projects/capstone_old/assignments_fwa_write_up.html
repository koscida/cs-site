FWA Write-Up

Greenpeace – A New Warrior


I like the concept here and what they are trying to do with interactive designs. When displaying 3D modeling, there are very few programs out there that one can see the interior of a ship and the public has access to. Putting this on a website makes a bit slower, but the resolution available is amazing.

The navigation was a bit confusing, and I found there to be inconsistencies when I clicked or hovered over things. After I first clicked on the ship, I though additional clicks would take me to other pages, but they didn't. I also had to look for the navigation and how to get back to where I started, which was confusing. When one clicks to the ship decks, there is dual animation for the deck and the navigation, I lost track of the navigation and couldn't find it. It is also on the deck picture, blending with the background.

However, this was an excellent trade off because the oversized deck displays are great for viewing. Once one becomes used to navigating this new design, it becomes simple and very each to use. Overall, a great way is displaying ship information.
