Internet Art – Chapter 1


I thought the idea of cyberfeminism was an interesting think to come out of the internet, as a female in computer science, I get the sexual connotations technology and art can have. However, I might disagree with their message. Yes, I understand the bold statement they are trying to make and they definitely come off as in–your–face and resolute, which might have been necessary for the time, and certainly the type of message I would expect from women in that age group, but I'm not convinced if that was the best way to deal with cyber feminism. The eroticization of their message, I think, undermines it and does the opposite of their intent. As a female who's entire career will probable revolve around the internet, I do want to be treated equally in a male–dominated field, and I do want my opinions to be treated the the same amount of respect as a male colleague, however, calling myself the "future cunt" might not be the best way to approach this problem.
